## Vehicle Management System with Vehicle Maintenance

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/375671c862174ccd96608c820b39f71d/ghb20250917) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used to run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Change Password
3. Personal Information
4. Document Information Management
5. Vehicle Information Management
6. Business Document Management
7. Accident Registration Management
8. Maintenance Registration Management
9. Service Registration Management
10. Fuel Registration Management
11. Violation Information Management


**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (change password, personal information)
3. Employee Management
4. Document Information Management
5. Vehicle Information Management
6. Business Document Management
7. Accident Registration Management
8. Maintenance Registration Management
9. Service Registration Management
10. Fuel Registration Management
11. Violation Information Management
